​Unit 1 - The Online World

Understanding the Modern Online World

This unit focuses on understanding the modern online world and its impact on our daily lives. It introduces key concepts, such as how websites operate, the mechanics of email delivery, and the technology that powers online communication and data exchange.

Exploring Online Services and Technologies

Through this unit, I explored various online services, including:

  • Email: Its role in communication and how it functions behind the scenes.
  • Online Data Storage: Services like cloud platforms that allow secure and accessible file storage.
  • Collaborative Software: Tools like Google Docs or Microsoft Teams that enable teamwork and productivity.
  • Search Engines and Blogging: Their significance in information retrieval and content sharing.

This unit required independent research into the technology and software that support online services. For example, I studied the Internet of Things (IoT) and how it represents the next phase of technological evolution.

Unit 3 – A Digital Portfolio & Unit 13 – Website Development

These units focus on designing, creating, and reviewing a digital portfolio and a website portfolio.

The learning aims include:

  • Design a digital portfolio
  • Create & test a digital portfolio
  • Review the digital portfolio
  • Understand the uses and features of websites
  • Design and develop a website
  • Test and review the finished website

Project Overview

In Unit 3, we designed our digital portfolios through stages including:

  • Creating storyboards and structure charts.
  • Testing the portfolio with user feedback and reviewing it.

Digital Portfolio Structure

The portfolio included web pages structured as follows:

  • Home page: Contains an introduction and general information about the portfolio, including the name and a brief description.
  • Sections: Describe key achievements, skills, and experience, including "About Me" and "My Achievements."
  • Context Pages: Provide additional information and examples of work, including pages with projects and details about courses and certificates.

Designing the Website Portfolio

In Unit 13, we focused on creating our web portfolio, covering:

  • Designing the website to meet specific goals and requirements.
  • Developing and testing the website, ensuring functionality and usability across eight linked pages.

Review and Improvements

In both units, we emphasized:

  • Reviewing content to ensure it meets audience needs and design documentation.
  • Evaluating the effectiveness of the portfolios and websites based on user feedback.
  • Making valid suggestions for further improvements and refinements.
